Unlock All Levels

Go to the Settings Menu (from the level select screen). Touch and hold in the middle of the 'P' for 'PRUNE' for 5 seconds. You should hear a chime noise and the game will restart with all levels unlocked and available to play.


Complete Current Chapter

While at the level select screen, touch and hold the chapter number up at the top for 5 seconds. You should hear a chime noise and the game will restart with all the levels in the chapter completed.
